Character Bible

Main character


The main character is a monkey/human hybrid and this duality turns the game to become very interesting and challenging as the player have to change between monkey and human form to overcome obstacles.

  • HEALTH: 100HP
  • ATTACKS
    • SHOT: 20HP
    • DASH: 30HP

Human

The human has the advantage of being capable of attacking the enemies from a long distance by shooting is gun.

On the other hand he can't grab or climb the edges of the walls.

Monkey

The monkey is more agile and has the advantage of being capable of grabbing the edges and climb the walls.

His close range attack is stronger than the human shot. The dash can be also used to dodge some enemies attacks.

Enemies

There are threee types of enemies in the game. Their aspect is the same and they only change in size and abilities.

Itâ€™s important to note that enemies have a both vision range and vision angle, which allows the player to sneak up on them. The player also needs to be careful because they will help other enemies if they are attacked.

Despite these three types there is also a final boss that is only half implemented and doesn't appear in the first level.

Ranged

This enemy is capable of attacking from a long distance. They shoot from afar and try to escape the player when he gets to close.

  • HEALTH: 20HP
  • ATTACKS
    • SHOT: 20HP

Melee

This enemy actively follows the player and dashes to him to attack.

  • HEALTH: 30HP
  • ATTACKS
    • MELEE: 35HP

Hybrid

This last enemy combines the two attacks from the previous enemies. Besides that it is also bigger and stronger.

  • HEALTH: 80HP
  • ATTACKS
    • SHOT: 20HP
    • MELEE: 50HP

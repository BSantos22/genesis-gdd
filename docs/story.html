Story Bible

Story outline


Year 2480, the planet Earth is in danger, the Human race is in danger. Overpopulation, polution, resources exausted, the Humans need a new place where they can live. A solution is advanced, its name is Genesis, a new start for the Human race.


Genesis is a genetic and spacial project developed in order to create an hybrid of a human and a monkey, in order to obtain a being with super strength, agility e stamina, characteristics of the monkeys, while keeping the intelect of the human. The purpose of this project was to send this hybrids to the outer space to help discover new planets, where these hybrids, more resilientes, could better survive.


The first hybrid named Adao was sent to the planet Broccolis-X45, in the year 2487 in order to check the conditions of the planet, as well as prospect for new forms of life in the planet. However, when aproaching the orbit of the planet, the ship is atacked by inhabitants of the planet, making it to crash in the planet. Adao has now the task to explore the planet, while also surviving the menace respresentad by the hostiles inhabitants, called Broccolions led by Lord Commander Raditz.
